About AfriTech Agribusiness Loans

We provide access to loans to farmers who meet the following credentials:

  1. Must have 1-3 acres of land or above.
  2. Provide their Mpesa statement for the last year.
  3. Provide their Title Deed for confirmation on our end.
  4. Show past records about the farms yield.
